About Us

We’re building the infrastructure
Africa’s trade deserves.

Smart Trade Africa is a digital trade platform that unifies tax, payments, customs, compliance, and supply chain into one seamless experience for cross-border African commerce.

01 Our Story

Born from a simple question

Why is it easier for an African business to trade with Europe than with another African country?

Today, intra-African trade accounts for just 16% of the continent's total commerce — compared to 68% in Europe and 59% in Asia. The African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) was created to change this, but the digital infrastructure to make it real didn't exist.

Smart Trade Africa was built to fill that gap. We provide the technology layer that connects traders, governments, banks, customs, and logistics providers across Africa's 54 nations — making compliant cross-border trade as simple as domestic transactions.

02 What We Do

One platform. Every trade function.

We replace fragmented systems with a unified digital backbone:

⚡

Tax & Compliance

Automated duty calculations, real-time tax assessments, and digital compliance checks across all 54 AfCFTA member states.

💳

Payments & Settlement

Cross-border payments with instant FX settlement. Integrated with PAPSS for pan-African payment processing.

📦

Supply Chain

End-to-end shipment tracking, warehouse management, and customs clearance — from port to final destination.

🏛️

Government Intelligence

Real-time trade analytics, revenue dashboards, and policy tools for customs authorities and trade ministries.

🔗

Automated Ledger

Double-entry bookkeeping generated automatically from every trade event. Full audit trail, zero manual entry.

🛡️

Insurance & Risk

Trade credit insurance, cargo coverage, and risk scoring — integrated directly into the transaction flow.
